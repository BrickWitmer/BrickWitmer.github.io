Recipe for Scrabbled Egg

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs butter

Method

  1. Melt butter in a ferying pan over midium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted, add cream and eggs
    4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  4. When the eggs are still moist, remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
